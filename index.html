The reason I picked supply chain is kind of funny but also very on brand. Growing up, the first game I ever played was Minecraft, and the thing I got weirdly good at wasn’t fighting or building houses, it was building automated item transportation and storage systems. I’d make farms that produced items automatically, move them through elevators, and sort everything into labeled storage so I wouldn’t have to think about it later. At some point I realized that’s basically supply chain — moving stuff, organizing it, and making sure the right things end up in the right place without everything breaking. So I made the choice. Then once I actually started working in ERP systems and warehouse operations during my internships and co-op, it kind of clicked that this is actually how my brain works. I like building systems that make things run smoother and reduce manual work. So what started as a random choice turned into something I actually care about.
